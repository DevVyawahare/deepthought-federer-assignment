# Scraping Notes

No large-scale automated scraping was used.

Light automation experiments included:
- LinkedIn enrichment
- website metadata extraction
- basic company classification

Most qualification work was manual due to high false-positive risk.

---

# Why Heavy Automation Was Avoided

The ICP required nuanced judgment around:
- manufacturing legitimacy,
- ownership structure,
- technical differentiation,
- and growth signals.

These areas produced high hallucination/error rates when fully automated.

---

# AI Usage Philosophy

AI was used as:
- a research assistant,
- summarization layer,
- and organizational aid.

Human verification remained mandatory for all shortlisted companies.
