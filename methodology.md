# Research Methodology

## Objective
Identify 25 Indian specialty manufacturers matching the Federer ICP framework.

## Selected Geography
Hyderabad

## Selected Segments
- Specialty biotech
- Diagnostics & life-science tools

## Research Funnel

### Stage 1 — Broad Discovery
Sources used:
- LinkedIn
- BioSpectrum India
- IndiaMART
- DSIR databases
- Startup India
- Trade show exhibitor lists
- CDSCO
- ZaubaCorp
- Tofler

Initial universe:
~90 companies

---

### Stage 2 — Fast Elimination
Companies were rejected if they were:
- CROs
- distributors
- generic pharma
- PE-owned
- subsidiaries
- inactive
- non-manufacturers

Remaining:
~38 companies

---

### Stage 3 — Deep Qualification
Each company was evaluated across:
- manufacturing capability
- technical differentiation
- founder profile
- sector tailwinds
- growth signals

---

# AI Usage

AI was used for:
- brainstorming search terms
- summarizing websites
- organizing notes

AI output was NEVER trusted directly.

All important claims were manually verified using:
- company websites
- LinkedIn
- interviews
- certifications
- government databases

---

# Hallucination Guardrails

## Rule 1
No company accepted without manufacturing proof.

## Rule 2
Founder background manually verified.

## Rule 3
At least 2 independent sources per critical claim.

## Rule 4
Maintained explicit fail-list.

---

# Key Learnings

- Many biotech firms are actually service companies.
- Manufacturing verification is the hardest part.
- Founder quality strongly correlates with differentiation.
- Trade-show exhibitor lists are highly valuable sourcing datasets.
