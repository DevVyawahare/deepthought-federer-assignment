# Quality Control Framework

## Main Risks
- false positives
- AI hallucinations
- distributors posing as manufacturers
- stale companies
- ownership confusion

---

# Validation Rules

## Manufacturing Proof Required
Must verify:
- plant/factory
- production capability
- manufacturing language
- certifications

---

## Multi-Source Verification
No major claim accepted from one source only.

---

## Human Review Layer
Borderline companies manually reviewed.

---

## Confidence Scores

### High Confidence
Strong evidence across all core criteria.

### Medium Confidence
Some missing data but strong overall fit.

### Borderline
Needs further investigation.

---

# Rejection Discipline

Companies were conservatively rejected if:
- ownership was unclear,
- manufacturing evidence was weak,
- or activity signals were stale.
