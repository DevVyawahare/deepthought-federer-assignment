# 1000-Company Scale-Up Proposal

# Objective

Build a database of 1000 genuinely ICP-qualified specialty manufacturers in India within one month.

The focus is:
- promoter-led,
- technically differentiated,
- actively growing,
- India-based specialty manufacturers.

---

# Funnel Strategy

## Stage 1 — Raw Universe
Target:
15,000–20,000 companies

## Stage 2 — Automated Filtering
Reduce to:
3,000–4,000 companies

## Stage 3 — Human Qualification
Reduce to:
1,500–1,800 companies

## Stage 4 — Final Validation
Deliver:
1,000 high-confidence companies

---

# Week-by-Week Plan

## Week 1 — Data Collection

Sources:
- DSIR
- CDSCO
- IndiaMART
- TradeIndia
- LinkedIn
- export databases
- trade show exhibitor lists
- Startup India
- Tofler
- ZaubaCorp

Output:
15k–20k raw companies

---

## Week 2 — Automated Filtering

Remove:
- CROs
- distributors
- generic pharma
- PE-owned firms
- subsidiaries
- software-only companies

Use AI/NLP pipelines to classify:
- manufacturer vs service
- specialty vs commodity
- founder-led vs institutional

Output:
3k–4k probable candidates

---

## Week 3 — Human Qualification

Manually validate:
- manufacturing evidence
- founder quality
- growth activity
- differentiation
- ownership structure

Apply Federer scoring.

Output:
1,500–1,800 validated companies

---

## Week 4 — Quality Control

Perform:
- duplicate detection
- confidence scoring
- random audits
- borderline review

Finalize:
1000 high-confidence ICP companies

---

# Quality Control System

## Rule 1
No critical claim accepted from one source alone.

## Rule 2
Manufacturing proof mandatory.

## Rule 3
Human review for edge cases.

## Rule 4
Maintain explicit fail-list.

## Rule 5
Confidence tagging:
- High confidence
- Medium confidence
- Borderline

---

# Expected Funnel Yield

| Stage | Companies |
|---|---|
| Raw universe | 20,000 |

| Specialty filter | 3,500 |
| Ownership/activity filter | 1,800 |

---

# Key Insight

The hardest challenge is not sourcing companies.

The hardest challenge is:
- verifying real manufacturing,
- identifying differentiated capability,
- validating ownership,
- and avoiding false positives at scale.
