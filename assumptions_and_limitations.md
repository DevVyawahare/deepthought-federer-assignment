# Assumptions and Limitations

## Revenue Estimation
Many private companies do not disclose updated revenue publicly.

Revenue bands were estimated using:
- employee count,
- manufacturing scale,
- export activity,
- public filings,
- and media references.

---

## Founder Background Verification
Founder and decision-maker profiles were verified wherever publicly available using:
- LinkedIn,
- interviews,
- conference profiles,
- company websites.

Some smaller companies had limited public information.

---

## Manufacturing Verification
Manufacturing capability was accepted only when explicit evidence existed, including:
- factory mentions,
- facility photographs,
- GMP/ISO certifications,
- production descriptions,
- or regulatory approvals.

Companies without sufficient manufacturing evidence were conservatively excluded.

---

## Ownership Verification
PE ownership and subsidiary relationships were checked using:
- company filings,
- media reports,
- and corporate databases.

Some private ownership structures may not be fully transparent.

---

## AI Usage Disclaimer
AI tools were used for:
- research assistance,
- summarization,
- and organization.

No company was included solely based on AI-generated claims without independent verification.

---

## Time Constraint
Given the assignment timeline, some companies may require further validation through:
- MCA filings,
- direct outreach,
- or deeper financial verification.
